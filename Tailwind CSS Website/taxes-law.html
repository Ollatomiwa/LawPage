Landmark Tax Cases in Nigeria

2022 Case

MTN Nigeria vs FIRS

The Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) accused MTN of under-remitting taxes to the tune of $2 billion. The case centered on proper interpretation of tax treaties between Nigeria and South Africa.

Tax Treaty Interpretation
2020 Case

Shell Petroleum vs Lagos State

A landmark case on tax jurisdiction where the court ruled that states could collect consumption taxes from companies operating within their territories, even if registered elsewhere.

Tax Jurisdiction
2018 Case

PZ Cussons vs FIRS

This case clarified the tax treatment of royalty payments and technical service fees to foreign entities, establishing important precedents for transfer pricing regulations.

Key Tax Considerations

  • Nigeria operates a self-assessment system - taxpayers must compute and file accurate returns
  • Recent Finance Act amendments have introduced significant changes to tax laws
  • Tax disputes often arise from interpretation of statutes and applicable rates
  • Proper documentation is critical in defending tax positions
